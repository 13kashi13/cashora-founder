# Founder Photo Instructions

## Required Action
Place your founder photo in this directory as `founder.jpg`

## Photo Specifications

### Technical Requirements
- **Filename**: `founder.jpg`
- **Format**: JPEG or JPG
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:5 (portrait orientation)
- **Recommended Size**: 800x1000px minimum
- **File Size**: Under 500KB for optimal web performance

### Photo Style Guidelines
- **Lighting**: Soft, natural lighting (avoid harsh shadows)
- **Background**: Slightly blurred or clean background
- **Expression**: Confident, calm, approachable
- **Attire**: Professional but not overly formal
- **Framing**: Head and upper body visible
- **Quality**: High resolution, sharp focus

### Mood & Feel
The photo should convey:
- "A serious builder, not an influencer"
- Thoughtful and grounded
- Professional but human
- Trustworthy and authentic

### Post-Processing (Optional)
- Slight brightness adjustment if needed
- Subtle contrast enhancement
- Gentle background blur for depth
- NO heavy filters or artificial effects
